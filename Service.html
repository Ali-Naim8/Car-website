Business Goals


Welcome to Classic White! We are a family-owned business that has been serving the local community for over 25 years. Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable auto services at affordable prices, while delivering exceptional customer service.

Our team of experienced mechanics are ASE-certified and equipped with the latest diagnostic tools to ensure that your car is in top condition. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we have the knowledge and expertise to get the job done right the first time.

At Classic White, we believe in transparency and honesty. We provide our customers with detailed explanations of the work that needs to be done, along with an accurate estimate of the cost. We will never recommend unnecessary repairs or services, and we always strive to find the most cost-effective solution for our customers.

We are passionate about cars and take pride in our work. Our values of integrity, professionalism, and excellence are reflected in everything we do, from our top-notch services to our friendly and welcoming atmosphere.




Thank you for considering Classic White. We look forward to serving you and your vehicle with the highest level of care and expertise.
